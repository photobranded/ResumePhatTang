About Me

"A person who never made a mistake never tried anything new." - Albert Einstein

As a 1st generation immigrant in the US I have always been motivated to make my family proud by following my dreams. I moved to the US at the age of 10 from Germany and I am constantly amazed at how many opportunities open up to me as long as I work hard towards my goals and stay resilient.

On this journey I made a lot of great friends, co-workers, and mentors who helped shape me into the person I am today. As a kid, I was always curious to understand why and how things work in this world. Math came easy to me and through my education I was intrigued by my Physics, Chemistry, and Engineering classes. I continued my education in Community College and graduated from UCI with a Bachelor's of Science in Applied Physics with a concentration in Electrical Engineering. My next goal in my career is to find a company that allows me to use my resilience and creativity to overcome any challenge while giving me opportunities to broaden my knowledge in Science.
